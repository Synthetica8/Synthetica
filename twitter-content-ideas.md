# Twitter Content Strategy

## Daily Themes
- **Montag**: Tech News & Analysis 
- **Dienstag**: AI Development Tips
- **Mittwoch**: Industry Insights  
- **Donnerstag**: Tool Reviews & Recommendations
- **Freitag**: Future Predictions & Vision
- **Samstag**: Behind-the-scenes / Personal
- **Sonntag**: Community Questions & Engagement

## Hashtag Strategy
- **Core**: #AI #MachineLearning #TechNews
- **Trending**: Check daily trends
- **Niche**: #OpenSource #Automation #Future

## Timing (Europe/Zurich)
- **Peak**: 9:00, 12:00, 18:00, 21:00
- **Avoid**: 2:00-7:00 (low engagement)

## Content Sources
- Hacker News trending
- ArXiv AI papers
- Tech Twitter discussions  
- Personal experiments/insights
- Industry reports

## Engagement Tactics
- Reply within 30 mins to comments
- Quote tweet with added insight
- Join trending conversations early
- Share others' content with commentary
