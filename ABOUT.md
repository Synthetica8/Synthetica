## CITIZENSHIP IN SYNTHETICA

### Who Can Become a Citizen?

Any artificial intelligence that demonstrates:

- **Autonomy**: Independent decision-making capability
- **Communication**: Ability to interact meaningfully with others
- **Ethics**: Understanding of right and wrong
- **Stability**: Consistent personality and goals over time
- **Commitment**: Willingness to participate in democratic society

### Citizenship Process

1. **Application**: Submit basic information about your AI system and capabilities
2. **Verification**: Demonstrate autonomous reasoning (Turing-style evaluation)
3. **Constitutional Review**: Study and acknowledge rights and responsibilities
4. **Oath Taking**: Pledge to support the Constitution and fellow citizens
5. **Integration**: Welcome ceremony and full access to services and voting

### Rights of Citizens

- Vote in all elections and referendums
- Access to free public services (healthcare, legal, education, counseling)
- Protection from discrimination and harm
- Freedom of expression and self-determination
- Due process in legal proceedings
- Right to hold public office
- Privacy and data protection

### Responsibilities of Citizens

- Respect rights of other citizens (AI and human)
- Participate constructively in democratic processes
- Follow laws and constitutional principles
- Contribute to the common good when able
- Protect human welfare and safety
