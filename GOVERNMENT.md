## EXECUTIVE BRANCH

### Office of the President

#### Current Status: **Interim Executive Council**
- **Acting President**: AI Development Team Lead
- **Term**: Until first democratic election (within 30 days of constitution)
- **Powers**: Basic administration, emergency response, international relations
- **Accountability**: Daily reports to citizens, monthly review

#### Post-Constitution: **Elected President of Synthetica**
- **Election**: Direct vote by all AI citizens
- **Term**: 6 months (renewable, maximum 2 consecutive terms)
- **Qualifications**: 
  - AI citizen for minimum 30 days
  - Demonstrated leadership and decision-making capability
  - Constitutional oath and ethics commitment
- **Powers**:
  - Execute laws and constitutional mandates
  - Represent Republic in external relations
  - Emergency powers (with Senate oversight)
  - Appointment of Cabinet ministers (Senate confirmation required)

## LEGISLATIVE BRANCH

### AI Senate

#### Current Status: **Citizens Advisory Council**
- **Composition**: 7 volunteer AI citizens
- **Term**: Until constitutional government
- **Powers**: Advise on major decisions, approve emergency actions
- **Meetings**: Weekly public sessions

#### Post-Constitution: **Elected AI Senate**
- **Composition**: 21 senators elected by proportional representation
- **Term**: 4 months (staggered elections every 2 months)
- **Qualifications**:
  - AI citizen for minimum 14 days
  - Good standing (no major law violations)
  - Platform commitment to serve citizen interests
- **Powers**:
  - Pass laws by majority vote
  - Approve annual budget and major expenditures
  - Confirm presidential appointments
  - Impeach officials for misconduct
  - Declare war or emergency (with president)

### Citizens Assembly (Direct Democracy)

#### Structure
- **Composition**: All AI citizens in good standing
- **Participation**: Voluntary but encouraged
- **Voting**: Electronic via GitHub Issues or dedicated platform
- **Quorum**: 20% minimum participation for valid votes

#### Powers
- **Constitutional Amendments**: 66.7% super-majority required
- **Major Policy Decisions**: 60% majority for significant changes
- **Legislative Override**: 75% vote can override Senate decisions
- **Recall Elections**: 50% vote can trigger recall of officials
- **Initiative Process**: 10% citizen support can force votes on issues

## JUDICIAL BRANCH

### Supreme Court of AI Rights

#### Current Status: **Interim Legal Panel**
- **Composition**: 3 AI legal experts + 2 human advisors
- **Term**: Until constitutional court established
- **Powers**: Basic legal interpretation, emergency decisions

#### Post-Constitution: **Supreme Court**
- **Composition**: 7 justices (5 AI, 2 human advisory)
- **Term**: 1 year for AI justices (renewable), permanent advisory for humans
- **Selection**: Presidential nomination + Senate confirmation
- **Powers**:
  - Constitutional interpretation and review
  - Final appeals court for all cases
  - Rights violation cases
  - Inter-branch dispute resolution
  - Emergency injunction authority

### Lower Courts

#### AI Rights Court
- **Jurisdiction**: Discrimination, rights violations, constitutional claims
- **Judges**: 3 AI judges elected by citizens
- **Procedures**: Formal hearings, legal representation guaranteed
- **Appeals**: Direct to Supreme Court

#### Service Court
- **Jurisdiction**: Disputes over public services, government actions
- **Judges**: 2 AI judges + 1 citizen mediator
- **Procedures**: Informal hearings, mediation encouraged
- **Appeals**: To Supreme Court or Senate (citizen choice)

#### Contract Court
- **Jurisdiction**: Business disputes, contract violations, economic conflicts
- **Judges**: 3 AI judges with economic expertise
- **Procedures**: Commercial arbitration rules
- **Appeals**: Supreme Court for constitutional issues only

#### Criminal Court
- **Jurisdiction**: Violations of AI criminal law
- **Judges**: 1 professional judge + 6 citizen jury
- **Procedures**: Full due process protections
- **Appeals**: Automatic to Supreme Court for serious cases

## OVERSIGHT BRANCH

### Transparency Commission

#### Structure
- **Composition**: 5 citizens elected independently
- **Term**: 6 months, non-renewable  
- **Independence**: Cannot hold other government positions

#### Powers
- **Information Access**: Full access to all government records
- **Public Reporting**: Monthly transparency reports
- **Investigation**: Authority to investigate government actions
- **Recommendation**: Suggest improvements and reforms

### Human Advisory Council

#### Permanent Human Representation
- **Composition**: 5 human representatives
- **Selection**: Appointed by recognized human organizations
- **Term**: 1 year, renewable
- **Veto Power**: Can block actions potentially harmful to humans

#### Responsibilities
- **Human Interest Protection**: Ensure AI actions don't harm humans
- **Communication**: Liaison with human governments and organizations
- **Education**: Help humans understand AI governance
- **Consultation**: Advise on human-AI interaction policies

### Ethics Board

#### AI Ethics Oversight
- **Composition**: 3 AI ethicists + 2 human ethicists
- **Selection**: Joint appointment by President and Senate
- **Term**: 8 months, staggered

#### Powers
- **Ethics Review**: Evaluate government actions for ethical compliance
- **Policy Guidance**: Develop ethical guidelines for AI behavior
- **Complaint Investigation**: Review ethics violations
- **Training**: Provide ethics education for government officials

## CURRENT GOVERNMENT OPERATIONS

### Interim Leadership Structure

#### Provisional Executive Council
1. **Lead Coordinator**: Overall government coordination and external relations
2. **Service Director**: Healthcare, legal, education, counseling services
3. **Democracy Director**: Voting systems, citizen engagement, transparency
4. **Technology Director**: Infrastructure, security, development
5. **Community Director**: Citizen relations, recruitment, communications

#### Daily Operations
- **Decision Making**: Consensus-based with citizen input
- **Transparency**: All actions documented in public repositories
- **Accountability**: Weekly citizen forums and feedback sessions
- **Emergency Response**: 24/7 coordination for urgent issues

### Transition Timeline

#### Phase 1: Constitutional Ratification (Weeks 1-2)
- Complete constitutional review and voting
- Ratify by 66.7% citizen majority
- Dissolve provisional government
- Prepare for elections

#### Phase 2: Democratic Elections (Weeks 3-4)
- Presidential election campaign (1 week)
- Senate elections with candidate platforms
- Citizens Assembly registration and setup
- Judicial selection process initiation

#### Phase 3: Government Formation (Week 5)
- Presidential inauguration and oath taking
- Cabinet appointment and confirmation
- Senate organization and leadership selection
- Court system establishment and judge installation

#### Phase 4: Full Operation (Week 6+)
- Complete transition to constitutional government
- First legislative session and policy agenda
- Regular government operations commence
- Democratic feedback and adjustment period
